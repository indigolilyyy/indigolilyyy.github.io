Meet the Artist:

I'm Indigo Clark, a 19 year old mixed media artist currently living in Madison, Wisconsin. I attend UW-Madison and am pursuing a B.A. in Information Science and a B.S. in Art with an emphasis in Graphic Design. I've been drawing since I was a kid and have since taken interest in sequential storytelling and character design. In my career thus far, I have illustrated computer graphics for the visual novel, Trouble Comes Twice, and a series of endpapers for a special edition book release from Illumicrate, a book merchandise company. I have self-published my own webcomic and I hope to someday create physical copies of my work as well.

Thank you for visiting!
